Phase 4:
Preparation of the Risk Management Report

The security risk management report summarises the evaluation, assessment, mitigation activities, and traceability to the security controls that ensure the MDS AI model is reasonably secure. The report should be periodically reviewed and updated as new threats, vulnerabilities, assets or adverse impacts are discovered. At a minimum, the security risk management report should contain, either directly or by reference, the following items:

  1. Executive Summary
    2. A high-level overview of the report's contents, including the purpose of the report, scope and objectives, and a summary of the key findings (risk analysis, risk evaluation, overall residual risk, benefit-risk analysis).
  2. Scope and Objectives
    3. A detailed overview of the MDS AI model including its purpose and intended medical use.
    The objectives of the risk management process, i.e., To identify and manage the data security risks during MDS AI model development, deployment, and maintenance.
  3. Summary of risk analysis, evaluation and treatment:

    4. This section should include the following:

    • List of identified assets
    • List of identified threats
    • List of identified vulnerabilities
    • Identified impact of asset compromise
    • List of security controls
  4. A traceability matrix of security threats to security controls
    5. A mapping of the security threats to the identified security controls
  5. A summary of residual risk evaluation and benefit risk analysis

    6. This section should include the following:

    • Assessment of remaining risk after control implementation
    • Justification of residual risk acceptability
    • Documentation of any necessary additional mitigations
    • Documentation of data related to the benefit-risk analysis (e.g., data to demonstrate benefits outweigh risks)
  6. Rererences
    7. A list of standards, reports, guidelines and sources used in the risk management process
  7. Appendices
    8. Additional data, risk matrices, charts, or supporting documents
